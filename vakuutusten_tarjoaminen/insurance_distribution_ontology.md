## Core Entities

### InsuranceIntermediary
- **InsuranceBroker** (vakuutusmeklari) - Professional intermediary
- **InsuranceAgent** (asiamies) - Agent representing insurer
- **PartTimeAgent** (sivutoiminen vakuutusedustaja) - Ancillary activity

### Registration & Supervision
- **Registration** - Entry in Insurance Distribution Register
- **SupervisoryAuthority** - FIN-FSA (Finanssivalvonta)

### Requirements
- **GoodReputation** - No relevant convictions
- **ProfessionalQualification** - Exam + 15h/year training

---

## Key Rules Summary

| Rule | Condition | Conclusion | Legal Basis |
|------|-----------|------------|-------------|
| Broker Registration | Primary activity as broker | Must register | § 6 |
| Good Reputation | No relevant convictions (5 years) | Required | § 16 |
| Professional Competence | Exam or equivalent | Required | § 18 |
| Continuing Education | < 15 hours/year | Non-compliant | § 20 |
| Product Governance | Offers insurance | Must have system | § 42 |
| Liability Insurance | Broker registered | Min €1.56M required | § 58 |
| Asset Segregation | Holds client funds | Must segregate | § 59 |
| Compensation | Broker breach causes damage | Customer entitled | § 74 |

## Key Thresholds

| Item | Threshold |
|------|-----------|
| Part-time agent premium | €600/year or €200/person |
| Liability insurance minimum | €1,564,610/claim, €2,315,610/year |
| Continuing education | 15 hours/year |
| Good reputation | No relevant conviction 5 years (prison) or 3 years (fine) |
| Processing time | 3 months max |
