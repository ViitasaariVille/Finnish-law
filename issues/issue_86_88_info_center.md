## Gap Description

The DMN rules are missing coverage for **Sections 86-88** of the Traffic Insurance Act, which govern Liikennevakuutuskeskus's information center (tietokeskus) functions and data sharing requirements.

## Law Requirements

### §86 - Information Center (Tietokeskus)
- LVK acts as national information center (direktiivin tietokeskus)
- Collects and distributes data for victim protection
- Maintains: registration numbers, policy numbers, insurer contacts, exempt vehicles
- Cooperates with other EEA information centers
- 7-year data retention limit

### §87 - Additional LVK Duties
- Functions as national office for green card system
- Compiles traffic accident statistics (liikennevahinkotilasto)
- Compiles risk research by vehicle group (riskitutkimus)
- Authorizes representatives for green card agreements
- Issues border and transfer traffic insurances
- Cannot refuse border/transfer insurance requests

### §88 - Data Sharing with LVK
- Insurer data sharing with LVK for subrogation and enforcement
- Annual statistical data submission requirement
- Confidential data may be shared with LVK
